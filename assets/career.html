Educator at TERI Prakriti School

Educators at the school are people with primarily love for children as their first attribute. Given the focus on the kind of campus they are working; their love for nature is the only closest second. Respecting the planet and sustainable living practices, are integral to their nature and therefore, a positive attitude and deep knowledge of their subjects would be natural to them. Their approach is multi-sensorial and multidisciplinary. Above all, calm and smiling presence and a can do attitude results in a happy child whose learning curve is age appropriate and developmental.
