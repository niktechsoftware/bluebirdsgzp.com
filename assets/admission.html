Enroll in Class, Registration & Application Procedure

Registrations open on 1st January 2016 onwards

Fill Online Admission Form

The Prospectus-cum-Registration Form is available at our Admissions Office. Alternatively, you may fill the Registration Form online and then take the printout of the same. Please make sure that the form is duly filled and submit the printed copy of the same at our Admissions Office, along with the Prospectus-cum-Registration Fee. (See Contact page for Admissions Office address)

Parents are requested to submit the Application Form duly filled, along with copies of the following documents:

  • One passport-size photograph of the child.
  • Self attested copy of the birth certificate issued by the Municipal Committee/ Municipal Corporation.
  • Self attested copy of the residence proof.
  • Copy of progress report from previous school (If applicable).
